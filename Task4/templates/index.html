Traffic Accident Data Analysis

Analysis Summary

This report provides a visual analysis of traffic accident data, highlighting key patterns related to time of day, weather conditions, and road conditions. The visualizations below reveal the most common contributing factors to traffic incidents.

Accidents by Time of Day

This chart shows the distribution of accidents throughout the day. Analyzing this data can help in identifying peak accident times and implementing targeted safety measures.

Accidents by Weather Conditions

The weather plays a significant role in road safety. This visualization breaks down accidents by different weather conditions, such as rain, snow, and clear skies, to understand their impact.

Accidents by Road Conditions

Road conditions like wet, dry, or icy surfaces are major factors in accidents. This chart illustrates the frequency of accidents for each type of road condition.
